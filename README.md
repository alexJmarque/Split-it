Split It: Just Split It
Split It is an iOS app that makes splitting restaurant checks among friends fast, accurate, and stress-free.
Simply snap a photo of your receipt, select your items, and let the app calculate how much each person owes — including tax and tip. Unlike other apps, guests don’t need to sign up, making it the simplest way to settle the bill.
Features
Receipt Scanning – Upload a photo of your receipt to automatically extract itemized details
Item Selection – Each guest chooses what they ordered
Smart Splitting – Tax and tip are split proportionally across all items
No Accounts Needed – Guests can join without creating an account
